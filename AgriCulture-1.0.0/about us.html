About Kerala Disaster Relief Management

Our Mission

The Kerala Disaster Relief Management Portal is dedicated to supporting and strengthening the disaster preparedness and response mechanisms across Kerala. Our mission is to build a resilient community through coordinated volunteer networks, real-time communication, and efficient management of emergencies, especially floods.

The 2018 Kerala Floods

In August 2018, Kerala witnessed one of the worst floods in its history. Torrential rains caused unprecedented water levels in rivers leading to massive devastation. The floods claimed over 400 lives and displaced more than a million people. Entire towns were submerged, infrastructure was severely damaged, and recovery took months.

Despite heroic efforts by government agencies, the scale of the disaster exposed vulnerabilities in disaster preparedness and coordination challenges.

How This Website Helps During Flood-like Situations

Our platform serves as a central hub for disaster management in Kerala by:

  • ✓ Mobilizing trained volunteers quickly and efficiently across all 14 districts
  • ✓ Providing real-time alerts and emergency notifications to local communities and responders
  • ✓ Offering an interactive dashboard to monitor ongoing relief efforts and volunteer deployments
  • ✓ Facilitating communication between government agencies, NGOs, and grassroots volunteers
  • ✓ Educating citizens on flood preparedness, evacuation procedures, and safety guidelines

By harnessing technology and community strength, this portal improves response times, minimizes chaos, and ensures that help reaches those in need promptly, drastically reducing the impacts of disasters.
